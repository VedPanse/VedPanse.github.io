As a member of the Board of Directors of LEO Club, I have commited myself to social service: helping the underpriviledged people of India.




What is Leo Club?

Leo Club is an NGO based non-profit social service organisation, and is a subsidary of Lions Club. “Leadership, Experience, Opportunity.” That’s what makes a Leo. As the youngest members of Lions Clubs International, Leos embody the best qualities of our incredible organization. They are devoted young people who realize the power of action. Together, Leos and Lions form a powerful partnership — one of mutual respect where Lions learn from the innovative insights of Leos, and where Leos gain access to the proven strategies of those who’ve successfully served the world for decades.

Source: Leo Club Official Website




Why I chose Leo Club?

Leo Club is like a family to me. As a matter of fact, all the members of the Leo Club are about the same age as mine. When I first set my eyes on the motto of the Club, and the confidence and dedication of the members of the Club, I knew for a fact that I had to be a member of that Club. Not only did it provide me an opportunity to explore my potent 'Human" qualities, it also helped me in developing my leadership skills. Soon, I was promoted to the Board of Directors, and I knew I had taken the right decision by joining the club.




What is the purpose of this website?

Through this website, I will not only be archiving my memories with Leo Club, but will also be sharing my experiences with you. This will help us create more potential Leos.
