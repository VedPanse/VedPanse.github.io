Programming Dilemma



My opinions on where and how aspirant programmers should start, and remain productive throughout their journey.




Basics First

Before making an entry into computers by learning programming languages, you should have a strong base about how computers work. This knowledge will not only help you in your future projects, but will also help you understand the interaction between programming languages and the Operating System. Having general knowledge about the terminal, for example, will help to dive deeper into execution of code.
