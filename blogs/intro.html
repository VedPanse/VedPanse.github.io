INTRODUCTION

Hi, This is Ved.


Greetings, everyone! I am Ved Panse, a student at Woodridge High School in Aurangabad, India.


Mecca gate, Aurangabad. Credits: Wikimedia Commons


While Aurangabad may seem inconspicuous to some, it is famously known as the 'City of Gates,' boasting about 52 of them! Allow me to share a bit about what captivates me:


  • Computers: I aspire to pursue a career in this fascinating field.
  • Cricket: A sport I ardently follow.
  • Mathematics: The foundation of logical reasoning.


Beyond my academic pursuits, I am an avid food enthusiast, a trait inherited from my family. Exploring the diverse cuisine of India is a shared joy for us.


Did you know there are more than 800 sports in the world? While I can't play them all, the spirit of a sportsperson runs in my family. I've played three different sports at the district level and seven at the school level. If you're a fellow sports enthusiast, I'm always open to discussing sports. Feel free to reach out.
