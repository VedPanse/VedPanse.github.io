Implements decay, merge, and conflict resolution policies for stable long‑term context. Benchmarked on multi‑turn datasets with ablations on update frequency and redundancy. Cross‑platform assistant built with a gateway, typed services, and event queues. Real‑time sync over WebSockets with offline caching and conflict handling. Unified Gmail/Calendar/Tasks connectors with granular permissions. Focused on reliability, testability, and low‑latency UX.

C++ / OpenGL flight assistance system

Terrain‑aware synthetic vision using GPU‑accelerated rendering and stitched elevation data. Integrates sensor models and attitude to generate stabilized guidance overlays. Emphasis on numerical robustness, framerate stability, and low‑level profiling.
