Hi, I'm Ved Panse

Undergraduate at the University of California, San Diego, double majoring in Mathematics–Computer Science & Data Science. I research AI memory models and intelligent virtual assistants, aiming to build systems that remember and adapt over months, not minutes. Beyond research, I co-founded Falcon Eye — a synthetic-vision platform for aircraft that raised $1.2M and is being evaluated for integration into the HAL Tejas, India’s flagship 4.5-generation fighter jet. I bring the mindset of a builder-researcher-entrepreneur with a focus on real-world impact. Outside of work, you’ll usually find me reading or sketching ideas at Geisel Library, 2nd Floor.
